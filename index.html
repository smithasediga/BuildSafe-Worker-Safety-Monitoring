Common Construction Site Accidents

  • 🧱
    Falls from Heights
    Scaffolding, ladders, or rooftops — a major cause of injury and fatality.
  • 🚧
    Falling Objects
    Tools, materials, or debris dropping from above can cause severe injuries.
  • ⚡
    Electrical Hazards
    Contact with power lines, faulty tools, or exposed wiring.
  • 💥
    Equipment-Related Accidents
    Mishandling of cranes, bulldozers, or forklifts.
  • 🧯
    Fires & Explosions
    Caused by combustible materials or gas leaks.
  • 😷
    Respiratory Hazards
    Inhaling dust, fumes, or toxic chemicals on site.
